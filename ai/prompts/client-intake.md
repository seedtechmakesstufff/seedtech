1. **Create context nodes** — paste this doc into the AI Import field on `/admin/seo/context` → Service Nodes. Claude will generate structured context nodes from it.
2. **Seed keywords** — use Sections 9 + 5 + 7 as the basis for initial keyword research
3. **Scaffold pages** — use Section 7 to determine the page architecture
4. **Create strategy doc** — create an `audit_findings` strategy doc capturing the initial state and goals
5. **Configure business profile** — fill in `/admin/seo/context` → Business Profile from Sections 1–3
