# SeedTech New Client Website Onboarding Form
## Google Form Structure + Setup Guide

> **Purpose:** This document specifies the exact Google Form to build for new client website intake.
> The form covers everything needed to scaffold a new site on the SeedTech platform:
> pages, copy, SEO, integrations, brand, assets, and goals.
>
> **After submission:** Responses auto-populate a Google Doc template linked in the form confirmation.

---

## How to Build This in Google Forms

1. Go to **forms.google.com** → New Form
2. Title: **"SeedTech — New Website Onboarding"**
3. Description: *"Complete this form before your onboarding call. The more detail you provide, the faster we can build and the better your results. Budget ~30-45 minutes. You'll receive a link to a shared Google Doc after submitting where you can upload assets and add supplemental notes."*
4. Enable **"Collect email addresses"** in Settings
5. In Confirmation message: *"Thank you! We'll review your answers before your onboarding call. Here's your shared asset doc: [GOOGLE DOC LINK] — please upload your logo, photos, and any reference materials there."*

## Google Doc Asset Template

Create a Google Doc with this structure and link it in the form confirmation message:

```
# [CLIENT NAME] — SeedTech Onboarding Assets

## ① Logo Files
Upload here: PNG (transparent bg), SVG if available, any brand guidelines

## ② Team / Staff Photos
Upload here: Headshots, candid shots, job site/in-action photos

## ③ Location / Facility Photos
Upload here: Office, storefront, vehicles, equipment

## ④ Work Portfolio / Before & After
Upload here: Project photos, case study images

## ⑤ Existing Copy & Content
Upload here: Service descriptions, bios, brochures, pitch decks

## ⑥ Competitor Notes
Any intel on competitors beyond what's in the form

## ⑦ Supplemental Notes
Anything that didn't fit in the form
```

**Share settings:** "Anyone with the link can edit" — so the client can add files without needing a Google account.

## After Submission — Your Workflow

1. **AI Import** → paste Section 2–5 answers into `/admin/seo/context` → AI Import → Claude generates context nodes
2. **Business Profile** → fill in from Sections 1–3 in `/admin/seo/context`
3. **Seed keywords** → use Sections 10 + 5 + 7 answers as starting point
4. **Page architecture** → Section 7 drives page scaffold
5. **Competitor seed** → Section 10 competitors → `/admin/seo/competitors`
6. **GSC + GA4** → connect using IDs from Section 10–11
7. **Copy foundation** → Sections 2, 3, 5, 6, 8, 9 feed every page's AI prompts
